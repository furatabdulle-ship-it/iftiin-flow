ABOUT

Who I am when I bring all my worlds together

I move between consulting, education and artistic work with language. My background spans philosophy, educational sciences and several years in Data & AI consulting – working with large organisations while building spaces that centre BIPoC experiences.

  • Data & AI Enablement consultant for enterprise clients in Europe.
  • Founder of Iftiin Flow, a platform for digital & AI literacy.
  • Facilitator and lecturer on knowledge, power and technology.
  • Spoken word artist and community builder in Black and migrant contexts.
What this means for you

You don’t just get tool recommendations. You get someone who understands:

  • how organisations actually work,
  • how people learn and resist change,
  • and how power and bias show up in technology.

IFTIIN FLOW

Iftiin Flow – light and movement in the digital noise

Iftiin means “light” in Somali. Iftiin Flow is the place where clarity meets motion: a space for people who want to understand AI and digital technologies deeply enough to use them for liberation, not just efficiency.

Who it’s for

BIPoC professionals, social entrepreneurs and teams who:

  • want to work with AI without losing their values,
  • need orientation in a fragmented learning landscape,
  • and care about access, not gatekeeping.
What we do together
  • Build digital and AI literacy step by step.
  • Translate AI into your sector and daily work.
  • Design strategies and workflows that free up time and energy.
How it feels

Less overwhelm, more orientation. Less “everyone says I should use AI”, more “I know exactly what to learn next and why”.
